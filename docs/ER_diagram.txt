RELATIONSHIPS:
---------------------------------------------
1. departments  (1) ---- (n) employees
   - One department has many employees.
   - employees.dept_id → departments.dept_id

2. employees  (1) ---- (n) salary
   - One employee can have monthly salary records.
   - salary.emp_id → employees.emp_id

3. employees  (1) ---- (n) attendance
   - One employee has many attendance records.
   - attendance.emp_id → employees.emp_id

NOTES:
---------------------------------------------
- dept_id, emp_id, salary_id, att_id are primary keys.
- email is unique.
- salary table stores one row per month per employee.
- attendance.status must be P (Present), A (Absent), L (Leave).
